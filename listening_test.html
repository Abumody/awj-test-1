LISTENING 01

You are going to hear a story about an Omani ship.

Part One: Write short answers (not more than FOUR words)
1. What is the name of the ship?
2. When did the ship sail?
3. What country was the trip going to?
4. How many crew members jumped into the water?
Part Two: Choose the correct answer
5. The crew tried to signal for help using ______.
6. The members who stayed alive floated for ______ days.
7. They were rescued by ______ ship.
8. They stayed in Iran for ______ days.
