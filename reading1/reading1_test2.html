READING 02 (Items 1 - 5)

Read the texts. Are the statements which follow each text True or False?

1.
Do you use any of the social media applications, which are so popular these days? If you are younger, you may use My Space. However, adults are more likely to be found on Facebook and busy professionals may prefer something like LinkedIn.
My Space is the professionals' favourite application.
2.
Mr. and Mrs. Smith have one son and one daughter. The son's name is John. The daughter's name is Sarah. The Smiths live in a house. They have a living room. They watch TV in the living room. The father cooks food in the kitchen.
The Smith's family has two children.
3.
Idioms are short phrases that make language more expressive and interesting. One of the common idioms in English is "A dime a dozen". It means something that is very common and can be found everywhere.
Unknown or unusual thing is the meaning of "A dime a dozen".
4.
I am so glad that I decided to spend the semester here in Spain. We have an extended weekend coming up, so a group of my friends and I are going to travel to France for four days. It's so easy and cheap to travel internationally in Europe. I love it!
The writer is in France now.
5.
Madagascar has two seasons, a warm, wet season from November to April, and a cooler, dry season between May and October. However, different parts of the country have very different weather. The east coast is hotter and wetter.
Madagascar has cold weather in July and August.
