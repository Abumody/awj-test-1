READING 09 (Items 1 - 5)

Read the texts. Are the statements which follow each text True or False?

1.
Although, I like to read about successful people who managed their way from zero to hero, neither Bill Gates nor Steve Jobs were the biggest inspiration for me. My mother was the one who inspired me the most. I remember that she had two jobs at the same time, yet she found time to read bed-time stories to us every night.
The writer’s mother influenced him the most.
2.
I never imagined that my husband would remember my birthday. I thought he would forget as usual. But, to my surprise, he arrived home yesterday with a big box and said “Happy birthday my dearest wife”.
The wife was expecting a present from her husband.
3.
I have known Yasser since we were kids. He was our neighbour’s eldest son. Although, he was blind, Yasser never asked for help. He managed to do almost everything by himself. Yasser became blind as a result of a long illness.
Yasser was born blind.
4.
In my opinion, students should not be given any homework. It is as if we are loading them with extra work. I believe that they have a right to play and stop thinking about school once they get home. When I was young I always struggled to finish my homework.
The writer is against teachers giving students work to do at home.
5.
Oman has got lots of beautiful places for camping and hiking. Last Wednesday, my friend and I went to Jabal Shams. I could not believe it when I saw the summit covered in snow. Despite the fact it was very cold we spent one night and two days there. We saw some wild animals like foxes and eagles. We were very sad to leave as it was a truly wonderful experience.
The writer enjoyed the trip.
