READING 12 (Items 1 - 5)

Read the texts. Are the statements which follow each text True or False?

1.
The Empire State Building in New York was the tallest building in the world for over forty years, from 1932 until 1973. But what is truly amazing is the speed at which it was built. The work was finished in less than a year!
It took several years to build the Empire State Building.
2.
Born in Russia in 1891, Sergei Prokofiev showed an early talent for music. At the age of four, his mother began to teach him the piano. The following year, he composed his first piece of music. Then, when he was thirteen, he joined the National Music College.
Prokofiev started writing music when he was five.
3.
The paramedics helped the woman into the back of the ambulance. She needed urgent treatment, so I had to reach the hospital quickly. Then, at a crossroads, we were hit by a taxi! Fortunately, our vehicle was not badly damaged, and we got there in time!
The writer is a taxi driver.
4.
At Australian weddings, there is an unusual custom. On entering the hall, the guests are each given a polished stone, which they should then hold in their right hand throughout the ceremony. The idea is that this will help the couple to have a strong, long-lasting marriage.
Each guest should bring a stone to the wedding ceremony.
5.
Last month, South Carolina was hit by floods, which killed 25 people and left thousands of others homeless. Volunteers are now urgently needed to help us to repair the damage caused by this disaster. They should, if possible, have skills and experience in construction work.
Volunteers should be skilled in building houses.
