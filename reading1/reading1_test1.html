READING 01 (Items 1 - 5)

Read the texts. Are the statements which follow each text True or False?

1.
This year's Notting Hill Carnival, Europe's biggest street festival, was the best ever. Despite the cloudy skies, the carnival brought the streets to life in its own unique way with record numbers and little reported crime.
In Europe, there is a bigger street festival than Notting Hill Carnival.
2.

Please write to us and tell us about what food you like and don't like. This is our photo and please send us a photo of you to recognize you at the airport.
Sam is going to stay with a French family next month.
3.
Nowruz festival which is celebrated on 21st March is an official holiday. Most people celebrate this day by cleaning their homes, visiting their relatives and preparing different dishes. However, I enjoy going on a picnic on that day with my friends.
The writer spends the Nowruz day at home cooking.
4.
'Global Volunteers' is an official website that provides a lot of information about international volunteering projects. The applicants' age should be between 21 and 50 years. Also, they should choose the volunteering projects which suit their skills and abilities.
Teenagers and children can work with 'Global Volunteers'.
5.
The Palestine Museum of Natural History (PMNH) is a scientific institution with natural history collections. These collections include present and historical records of animals, plants, and more. The museum was founded in 2013. However, it was opened to the public in 2017.
The Palestine Museum started welcoming visitors in 2017.
